This ain't no ordinary spagget. It's the FILIPINO Spagett. Its SwEET, literally.

Ingredients

  • Sphaghetti Noodles
  • Beef Mince
  • Tender Juicy Hotdogs
  • Any Luncheon Meats
  • Sweet Filipino Sphaggeti Sauce (UFC/Del Monte)
  • Aromatics: Garlic, Onions
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

There isn't as many ingredients but the main important one is the Sweet Filipino Sphagetti Sauce specifically. IT IS A MUST.

Steps

  1. Boil the sphagetti noodles, depending on it's type that you brought.
  2. In a seperate pot/pan, saute the aromatics and ground pork.
  3. Add the hotdog and other luncheon meats.
  4. Add the sauce to the sauted aromatics and meats.
  5. Once cooked, combine the cooked noodles and sauce; put the shredded cheddar cheese on top and enjoy~!
