Eggs, eggs, and more eggs. That's why we love frittatas.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 Bacon Slices
  • 6 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Melted Butter
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup of Chopped Green Onions
  • 1 Cup of Shredded Cheddar Cheese

That's the general gist of ingredients, you can add or remove ingredients depending on your liking or availability of ingredients.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Cook bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk wet ingredients, then add in dry ingredients, and whisk until evenly mixed.
  4. Bake and serve!
